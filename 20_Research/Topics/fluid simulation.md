# Fluid Simulation

Physics-based simulation of liquids and gases for computer graphics. Methods include Eulerian (grid-based), Lagrangian (particle-based like [[SPH]] and [[MPM]]), and hybrid approaches (FLIP/PIC, APIC).

## Key Concepts
- Navier-Stokes equations (incompressible / compressible)
- Pressure projection and divergence-free velocity fields
- Free surface tracking: level sets, volume-of-fluid, particles
- Boundary conditions: solid walls, open boundaries ([[PML]])
- Turbulence: vorticity confinement, wavelet methods
- Two-phase and multi-material flows
