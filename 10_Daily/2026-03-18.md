## Simulation & Physics-Based Animation

**4. Robust Treatment of Collisions, Contact and Friction for Cloth Animation** `score 14.65` · TOG 2002 — Classic
*Bridson, Fedkiw, Anderson* · `10.1145/566654.566623`
Foundational [[cloth simulation]] [[contact]] algorithm. Models [[cloth]] thickness, works atop any dynamics solver. Still the baseline for [[IPC]] and contact-mechanics literature.

---

**5. GPU Multilevel Additive Schwarz Preconditioner** `score 14.4` · TOG 2022
*Wu, Wang, Huamin Wang* · `10.1145/3528223.3530085`
[[GPU simulation]] preconditioner using Morton-code multilevel domains. ~4× [[PCG]] speedup. Scales to 500K vertices. Compatible with L-BFGS, gradient descent.
