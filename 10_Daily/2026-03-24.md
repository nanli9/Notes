*Zhengye Pan, Jianwei Zuo, Jiajia Luo*
**Tag:** `arxiv:2603.21020` · `/paper-analyze-graphics arxiv:2603.21020`

Users can directly control energy/momenta for vivid [[deformable body]] and global motions — useful for games and VR. Most simulation-relevant paper in today's batch.
