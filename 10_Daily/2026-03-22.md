## Simulation & Physics-Based Animation

**Subspace Simulation in Multiple Domains** `10.1145/2816795.2818065` (Wu et al., 2015) — Domain decomposition for [[reduced-order]] [[deformable body]] simulation with coupling elements. Single linear solve, no stiffness tuning.
