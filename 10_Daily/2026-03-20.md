## Simulation & Physics-Based Animation

**4. CK-MPM: A Compact-Kernel Material Point Method** `score 22.65` · TOG 2025
*Liu, Wang, Li* · `10.1145/3731155`
Novel C²-continuous compact kernel for [[MPM]] using a dual-grid framework. Only doubles grid nodes per particle vs. linear kernels (vs. quadratic B-spline's larger stencil). Less numerical diffusion, faster runtime. Implemented in Taichi.

---

**5. Dress-1-to-3: Single Image to Simulation-Ready 3D Outfit** `score 22.4` · TOG 2025
*Li, Yu, Du, Jiang, Xie, Chen, Yang, Jiang* · `10.1145/3731177`
Pipeline from single image → separated garments with sewing patterns, optimized via differentiable [[cloth simulation]]. Combines diffusion priors for multi-view generation with differentiable physics for geometric refinement. Project: dress-1-to-3.github.io

---

**6. A Vortex Particle-on-Mesh Method for Soap Film Simulation** `score 20.9` · TOG 2024
*Tao, Ruan, Deng, Zhu, Wang, Chen* · `10.1145/3658165`
Hybrid particle-mesh vortex method for [[thin shell|thin film]] dynamics. Decomposes tangential velocity into circulation and dilatation components. Captures cascading vortical structures, surfactant dynamics, and gravity interactions on deforming soap films.

---

**7. High-performance CPU Cloth Simulation Using Domain-decomposed Projective Dynamics** `score 20.4` · TOG 2025
*Lu, Liu, Lan, Wang, Ishiwaka, Jiang, Wu, Yang* · `10.1145/3731182`
CPU-only [[cloth simulation]] matching GPU performance via domain decomposition of projective dynamics. Partitions garment into sub-meshes assigned to CPU cores. Key insight: balance parallelization granularity with convergence — fewer but larger domains than GPU threads.

---

**8. A Semi-implicit Material Point Method for Granular Materials** `score 19.9` · TOG 2016
*Daviet, Bertails-Descoubes* · `10.1145/2897824.2925877`
Exact nonsmooth Drucker-Prager yield criterion for granular [[MPM]] with varying volume fraction. Semi-implicit time-stepping preserves normal-tangential stress coupling. Validated on silo discharge and column collapse. Same group as the Coulomb friction cloth paper.

---

**9. Methodology for Assessing Mesh-Based Contact Point Methods** `score 19.4` · TOG 2018
*Erleben* · `10.1145/3096239`
Taxonomy and benchmark for [[contact]] point generation methods. Establishes test suite for evaluating quality of mesh-based contact detection, particularly for objects with sharp edges. Concludes local methods are insufficient — motivates semi-local approaches.

---

**10. A Semi-Lagrangian Contouring Method for Fluid Simulation** `score 18.9` · TOG 2006
*Bargteil, Goktekin, O'Brien, Strain* · `10.1145/1122501.1122503`
Explicit polygonal mesh surface tracking for [[fluid simulation]] using semi-Lagrangian advection of signed distance. Octree spatial index. Enables tracking surface characteristics (color, texture) at negligible cost.

---

**11. Deformation Embedding for Point-Based Elastoplastic Simulation** `score 18.65` · TOG 2014
*Jones, Ward, Jallepalli, Perenia, Bargteil* · `10.1145/2560795`
Point-based elastoplastic simulation via "embedded space" — least-squares best fit of rest state into 3D. Nearest-neighbor queries in embedded space replace remeshing for plastic flow. Handles simultaneous extreme elastic and plastic deformations.
